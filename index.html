Backend (advanced)

Demo mode (mask names)

Replaces every real person's name with a stable fake one (e.g. Torres, K.) across the pool, board, sheets and logs — so you can screen-share or pitch the tool to your company without exposing anyone. The same real name always maps to the same fake name. Turn off to show real names again. Nothing is changed in your saved data.
